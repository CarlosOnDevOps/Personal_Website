Profile

My interest in IT and the cloud began in 2016 when I wondered how in the world the Google Drive and iCloud software really worked. I quickly learned that the internet is an enormous cluster of smaller networks (subnets) with even smaller virtual networks (vNets) sending data to and from different networks flawlessly in fractions of a second. Soon, I began to home-lab and spin up virtual machines and servers with different operating systems, deploy containerized images, break critical Identity and Access Management rules and workflows then repair it. My security skills and knowledge have been built upon my fundamental understanding of networks and the internet. Now, I am able to operate within cloud platforms and tinker with all sorts of technologies!
